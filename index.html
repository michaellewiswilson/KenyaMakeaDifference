Our hearts go out to Kenya during this time of rebuilding after the tragic event at Westgate.

Although the 2014 trip will not be staying in Nairobi, it does raise great concern for us. We will be respecting federal and university safety rules regulating travel to Kenya, putting travel safety at highest prioriry. Currently, we are still planning to continue with the 2014 trip, changes to this plan will be posted.

We hope that Kenya will quickly return to the beautiful and peaceful country we have experienced in the past.
